Islami Bank Institute of Technology, Savar.

Islam Bank Institute of Technology, Savar is a humanitarian and non-profit organization with a different horizon and unique name in the world of technical education. Islami Bank Bangladesh Limited's human welfare organization the company started a journey from Dhaka city in 1995 with the approval of the Bangladesh government under the Islami Bank Foundation. To create qualified, honest, and ideal citizens with technical knowledge all over the country the institution has established six campuses. Later in 2001, with the approval of the 4-year Diploma in Engineering course from the Bangladesh Technical Education Board, it has been able to gain considerable popularity, confidence and reputation across the country by providing quality education to the students and has been acclaimed by the public at various levels as an ideal educational institution. In this institution, students get a basic knowledge of ethics besides technical education. At the present computer, electrical civil and Textile technology is being operated in this institution. In addition, plans have been made to introduce new courses suitable for the current era.
